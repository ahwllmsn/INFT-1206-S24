Three Fun Activities You Can Do This Summer

1. Host a Picnic

There's no better way to spend time with your friends this summer than to host a picnic with them!

There's no wrong answers for what dish you should bring, but here's a few ideas:

  • Pasta salad
  • Pies made with your favourite seasonal fruit
  • Sangria
  • A charcuterie board
  • Chicken wings
  • Watermelon slices

2. Go to the Beach

Take advantage of the summer heat by visiting a local beach with your friends!

Before you go, make sure that you remember to bring the essentials with you:

  • Sunglasses
  • Sunscreen
  • Towels
  • Flip-flops
  • A water bottle (stay hydrated!)
  • Swimsuit

3. Search for the Holy Grail

Studies show that the best way to spend your summer is within the cinematic universe of Monty Python and the Holy Grail (1975).

Before you embark on your quest, be sure to bring the following with you:

  • One shrubbery
  • Killer rabbit repellent
  • The knowledge of the average airspeed velocity of an unladen swallow (about 24 mph)
  • Your dead (bring them out when instructed)
  • Two coconut halves
